## Articles & Distill Publications

### A Gentle Introduction to Graph Neural Networks
**Citation**: Sanchez-Lengeling, B., Reif, E., Pearce, A., & Wiltschko, A. B. (2021). A gentle introduction to graph neural networks. *Distill*.

**Key Concepts**:
- **Graph Representation**: $G = (V, E)$ with attributes on nodes $V$, edges $E$, and global $U$.
- **Tasks**: Node-level, Edge-level, and Graph-level prediction.
- **Pooling**: Aggregating information from nodes to edges, or edges to global types.
- **Equivariance**: GNNs must be invariant to the ordering of nodes (permutation invariance).
